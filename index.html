Our vision

As an educational institution De Nobili Schools aim at preparing students to become intellectually competent, morally sound, spiritually oriented, willing to serve, committed to doing justice and open to further growth.

Our Mission


To form students:

l To become mature and spiritually oriented men and women of character.

l To strive after excellence in every field.

l To value and judiciously use their freedom.

l To be clear and firm on principles and courageous in action.

l To become agents of much needed social change in our country.

l To become men and women for others.
